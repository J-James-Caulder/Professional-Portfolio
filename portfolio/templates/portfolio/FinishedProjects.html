A Shadowing Flame
Hardcopy and E-book
Royalty always seems to have so many tough choices, high up in the castle, with servants to wait on them. However, Princess Rose has never been one to want others to serve her. But when several shadowy figures seem to want the princess out of the picture, she has no choice but to leave her fate in the hands of a mysterious newcomer, whose very existence is forbidden.
